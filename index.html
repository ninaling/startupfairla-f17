Startup Fair LA

ABOUT

Startup Fair LA, previously named Bruin Startup Fair, aims to connect UCLA’s best and brightest students to premier rising startups in the Los Angeles area. It’s a great way for entrepreneurial-minded students to interact with companies, offering an excellent opportunity for students to find full-time positions and internships in a close-knit startup environment. We hope to see you there!

In addition, this will serve as an incredibly exciting opportunity for recruiters to spread the word about your companies. This is one of the few startup-exclusive events at UCLA, so take advantage of this chance to recruit some of the brightest minds in the area!

Registration for students will open soon! Keep an eye out for our Facebook event. For more info, contact us at info@startupfair.la.
